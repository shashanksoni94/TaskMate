About Us





Taskmate allows users to create lists of goals that they want to accomplish (ex. A travel list, a career list, a events list) and share it with their friends and followers. Users will be able to view each others goals, cheer each other on, and work together to achieve their goals.

Key Features

  • Being able to create an account.
  • Being able to create lists filled with goals.
  • A newsfeed that alerts you when the people you are following (your friends) create and complete goals.
  • Being able to comment on and like goals and lists.
  • Being able to post pictures of the completed goals.
  • Having private / public goals.
  • Being able to search for users/goals/lists.
  • Group goals.
